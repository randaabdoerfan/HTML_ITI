About Us

ITI is one of the training entities affiliated with the Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT). The Institute develops youth capabilities and skills through a diversified set of capacity-building programs and professional advanced coaching. Advancing the productivity and efficiency of IT users in various businesses for work automation, promotion and facilitation. ITI has kept updating its training portfolio through continuous monitoring. Its main mission revolves around ICT human capacity development. Enriching the knowledge of University undergraduates in niche IT areas of high market demand. Graduating young cadres especially in niche areas of high demand in the ICT market. There are three types of goals- process, performance, and outcome goals. Process goals are specific actions or 'processes' of performing.
